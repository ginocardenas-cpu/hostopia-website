**Content**:

The first half of 2025 saw a "tidal wave" of ISP M&A activity, including $34.5 billion in cable mergers, as smaller operators recognized they couldn't survive the speed-and-price race alone.

The operators that are breaking this cycle aren't building faster networks — they're building deeper customer relationships by becoming the SMB's digital services partner, not just their bandwidth provider.
