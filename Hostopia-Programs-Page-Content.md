# CROSS-PAGE SEO NOTES

## Internal Linking Strategy
- Each Programs page links to at least 2 other Programs pages
- All pages link back to the Programs hub
- Link to Platform page when referencing provisioning or billing
- Link to Products when specific services are mentioned (email, websites, etc.)
- Cross-link Project Management ↔ Support (migration handoffs)
- Cross-link Channel as a Service ↔ Support (upsell/retention)
- Cross-link Partner Marketing ↔ Channel as a Service (campaigns feeding the channel)

## Schema Markup Recommendations
- **FAQPage schema** on Support page
- **Service schema** on each Programs page
- **BreadcrumbList schema**: Home > Programs > [Page Name]
