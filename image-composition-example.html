Hostopia Image Composition Guide

How to select images that speak to service providers while showing SMB outcomes

The Three-Layer Framework

Layer 1 — Foreground
Service Provider
The hero. The decision-maker. Always in focus.
Layer 2 — On Screen
SMB Outcome
The result. Visible on a device or in the background.
Layer 3 — Implied
Hostopia
The invisible enabler. Never shown. Always understood.

Applying This Across Product Pages

What to search for on stock photo sites and what to avoid

Ecommerce

Two professionals reviewing an online store on a tablet. Store visible on screen, conversation is the subject.

Search: "business tablet ecommerce demo" Avoid: "online shopping woman phone"

Business Email

Professional at a workstation reviewing email platform dashboard showing mailbox counts and uptime metrics.

Search: "IT manager email dashboard" Avoid: "person reading email on phone"

Website Builder

Service provider rep and SMB owner looking at a website preview together on a laptop. The website is the context, the partnership is the story.

Search: "client website review meeting" Avoid: "woman building website alone"

SEO / Digital Marketing

Professional presenting search performance results — Google Analytics or ranking report visible on a large screen.

Search: "SEO analytics presentation" Avoid: "magnifying glass on search bar"

Channel as a Service

Sales consultant at a modern workstation on a call, with CRM pipeline dashboard visible on their screen.

Search: "sales team CRM dashboard" Avoid: "call center rows of agents"

Support

Professional support agent at a clean desk with headset, focused and calm. Ticketing system visible on screen.

Search: "IT support professional workstation" Avoid: "smiling woman with headset"
